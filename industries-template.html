Our process

The first step is to understand in which phase of DEI maturity you are in. Each phase has different objectives and steps to obtain success in your DEI implementation.

Take our 5 min DEI assessment quiz to know where you stand. Find out my DEI maturity stage

  • Aware

    Aware DEI is new to my organization, and we are just becoming aware of its importance.

  • Compliant

    Compliant DEI in my organization is focused on compliance with EEOC and other legal requirements.

  • Tactical

    Tactical DEI has been connected to business initiatives and outcomes in pockets of the organization.

  • Integrated

    Integrated DEI is part of everything we do as an organization; we have both internal and external efforts on DEI.

  • Sustainable

    Sustainable DEI efforts are best in class and remain strong over time through our efforts to continuously improve and evolve.

Aware and Compliant implementation steps

Many organizations that come to Elance are in the aware and compliant stages of EDI.

In this stage, we prioritize connecting the leadership team to DEI personally and professionally as a way to increase retention, facilitate hiring, and increase profits.

We work with you to achieve quick success (1 to 6 months) in solving a business objective with DEI.
  • Upper management and CEO support DEI
  • DEI aligns with business strategy and objectives are set
  • A DEI owner or committee is appointed
  • An action plan is developped
  • The action plan is implemented, measured and iterated
  • Find success & grow DEI
