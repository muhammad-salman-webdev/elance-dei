Elance DEI Basics Training

Our DEI Basics program is the best way to initiate meaningful change within your company.
  • Non-oppressive, allowing individuals to engage in a judgment-free environment.
  • Quick and efficient (2 - 7 miute micro-capsules)
  • Start on the same foundational principles & Speak the same language

Whether you're a small start-up or a large corporation, our DEI Basics is built for organizations that are eager to increase awareness and promote diversity, equity, and inclusion within their teams.
